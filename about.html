Weisong Zhao, Ph.D.

Weisong Zhao (赵唯淞), Assistant professor (AP) at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT). He is building advanced optical microscopy for biomedical applications, as well as developing algorithms for low/high level computer-vision and bioinformatics. Excited about machine learning, open-source, and everything related to image.
