Principal investigator

Weisong Zhao (赵唯淞), Ph.D.

Professor at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT). His lab is building advanced optical microscopy for biomedical applications, as well as developing smart algorithms across modalities including optical microscopy, acoustic/photoacoustic imaging, and cryo-EM/ET for imaging and downstream analyses. All related to biomedical imaging.
